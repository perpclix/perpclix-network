# PERPCLIX

> Perpetual Futures for Every Solana Token.

Perpclix is a permissionless perpetual futures protocol that enables anyone to launch leveraged markets for any Solana token in as little as 60 seconds.

Built on Solana and inspired by open-source perpetual infrastructure, Perpclix transforms every token into a tradable derivatives market with transparent execution, decentralized settlement, and permissionless market creation.

---

# 🚀 What is Perpclix?

Launching perpetual futures markets traditionally requires centralized exchanges, governance approvals, or complex infrastructure.

Perpclix removes those barriers.

Creators can deploy perpetual markets for their tokens instantly, while traders gain access to leveraged long and short positions on emerging assets—all fully on-chain.

```text
Launch Token
      ↓
Create Perp Market
      ↓
Provide Liquidity
      ↓
Open Leveraged Positions
      ↓
Trade & Settle On-Chain
```

Every token.

One protocol.

Infinite markets.
